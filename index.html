  • Sell-side mandate management

Private Equity & Venture Capital

We connect growth-stage and mature businesses with institutional investors, family offices, and strategic investors through structured PE/VC mandates.

  • Growth equity fundraising
  • Structured investment teaser & CIM preparation
  • Investor roadshow management

Debt Advisory & Syndication

Structuring and sourcing optimal debt solutions from banks, NBFCs, and capital market participants to fund business growth.

  • Term loan syndication
  • NCD/bond issuance advisory
  • Working capital solutions
  • External Commercial Borrowings (ECB)
  • FCCB advisory

Valuations

Independent, defensible valuations prepared for transactional, regulatory, and financial reporting purposes.

  • Business enterprise valuation
  • ESOP & sweat equity valuation
  • Fair value for M&A transactions
  • Valuation for FEMA/NCLT purposes
  • Intangible asset valuation
